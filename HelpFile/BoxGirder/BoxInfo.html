Box Girder Information

 Box Girder Section Properties

The program "BoxGir" calculates the section properties for a box girder.
 

Input

Description:

a) IDENT: Identification Number (4-character maximum)
b) PROBS: Number of Problems.

Grid:

a) No: Problem Number
b) D: Depth of girder in feet
c) T: Enter the thickness of the web in inches.
d) No of webs: Enter the number of webs.
e) W: width of box section in feet.
f) ST: slab thickness on top in inches.
g) SB: bottom slab thickness in inches.
h) FT: fellet dimension on top in inches.
i) FB: bottom fillet dimension in inches.
Cantilevers:
j) EL: Length of cantilever in feet.
k) CL: Thickness of cantilever at the end in inches.
l) BL: Thickness of cantilever at the base in feet.
 

Output

The section properties calculated are moment of inertia (I-ft4), the location of the neutral axis from the top and bottom-(ft), area of the cross section (ft2), weight per ft (K/ft), and moment areas (top and bottom) Ft3.
