Trssmem Member Section Properties

The program Trssmem calculates the section properties of a truss member.

Input

Data may contain one or more problems.
All dimensions are entered in inches.

Description

a) Side Plate Width
b) Side Plate Thickness
c) Total Width (Outside Dims)    
d) First Holes Per Plate
e) Cover Plate Width
f) Cover Plate Thickness
g) H. Holes Per Plate
h) Number of Side Plates Other Than 2
i) Number of Cover Plates Other Than 2

Output:

Areas in inches2, radium of gyration in inch, moment of inertias in inches4, weight in #/ft. and widths of side and cover plates in inches.
