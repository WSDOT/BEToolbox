Step 2 - Define roadway section and superelevation profile

Check the "Correct for superelevation or crown" box if superelevation corrections are desired.

 

 

Offset from crown to profile grade line: Enter the offset from the crown point line to the profile grade line.

 

Superelevation Data: Define the superelevation profile be defining the left and right crown slope at three stations. Crown slopes are negative if the slope is down and away from the crown point and are positive if it is slope up and away from the crown point.
