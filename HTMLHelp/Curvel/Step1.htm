Step 1 - Define Vertical Curve

Vertical Curve Data

g1

Grade leading into the curve (%)

g2

Grade out of the curve (%)

Curve Length

Length of curve (ft)

PVI Station

Station of the point of vertical intersection of the entry and exit tangents to the curve

PVI Elevation

Elevation of the PVI Point (ft)

 

Only one vertical curve is used at a time. For a straight grade, list the PVI elevation for a convenient point on the grade and list the curve length as 0.
