UltCol

UltCol is a simple utility program for computing the ultimate moment capacity of a circular reinforced concrete column. The moment capacity is computed for various levels of axial force between the compression and tension limits. Tabular results and a moment interaction diagram are plotted.

Limitations and Assumptions

UltCol uses a simplified solution for estimating the ultimate capacity of a circular reinforced concrete column. Engineering judgement must be used when determining the applicability of this software to a particular situation.

 

The following assumptions have been made:

  • The ultimate concrete crushing strain is 0.003

  • The modulus of elasticity of reinforcing steel is 29,000 ksi

  • The reinforcing steel has a bi-linear, elastic-perfectly plastic, stress-strain relationship

  • The reinforcing steel is closely spaced and can be approximated by a thin steel tube
