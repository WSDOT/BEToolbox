GirComp

GirComp is a program that calculations the section properties of a composite girder with either a rolled or built-up steel section.

Input

Use the input grid to enter the dimensions and modular ratio for the composite girder.

 

Type

Select the type of composite section. Options are Rolled, General, and Built-Up. Rolled sections are one of the AISC W-shape girders. General sections do not have a specific shape and are defined by their overall geometric properties. Built-up shapes are defined by their top and bottom flange and web plates.

Cross sections can be added by pressing the + button. Select a row by clicking on the row header in the left-most column and pressing the - button to delete a cross section.

 

Select US or SI units.

 

Press the Update button to update the output listing.

Output

The section properties are listed in the report window.

 

The output for Rolled Beam and General Section problems consists of the moment of inertia, top and bottom section modulii, and location of the centroidal axis of the composite section for the modular ratios N1, N2, and N3. The first moment of area of the slab, Q, is also reported.

 

The output for Built-up problems consists of the moment of inertia, top and bottom section modulii, and location of the centroidal axis of the composite section for the modular ratios N1, N2, and N3. The first moment of area, Q, of bottom flange, slab, and, slab and top flange are also reported.
